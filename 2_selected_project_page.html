Financial Eligibility

Sr. No. Eligibility Point Satisfy
1 The tenderer should have an annual turnover of Rs 10 crores in each of the previous three financial years.
2 The bidder should be a profit-making firm and should not have made losses in the last two financial years out of the last five financial years.
3 The bidder must provide a copy of a bank certificate to prove the available credit limit and evidence of liquid cash assets to verify the working capital requirement.
4 The bidder should possess adequate working capital of more than 10% of the probable estimated cost of work for which they are bidding.
5 The contractor must have a minimum turnover related to fire alarm system projects in previous years.
